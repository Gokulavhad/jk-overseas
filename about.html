Company Overview

Pune's Precision Tooling Pioneer

JK Overseas was established in 2008 by industry veterans with a singular focus: bring world-class tooling quality to the Indian market and beyond. Starting with boring bars, we have grown into a comprehensive industrial tooling manufacturer serving 30+ countries.

Our 18,000 sq. ft. manufacturing facility in Moshi, Pune houses CNC grinding centres, CMM inspection equipment, heat treatment furnaces, and a fully equipped R&D lab. Every tool that leaves our facility is inspected against dimensional drawings and material certificates.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the most trusted name in precision tooling across emerging markets.

We envision a world where Indian engineering excellence is the global benchmark — where "Made in Pune" on a boring bar means the same as the finest European tooling, at a fraction of the cost. By 2030, we aim to be present in 60 countries with an expanded product portfolio covering the full spectrum of cutting tools.

Our Mission

Deliver precision tools that reduce cost-per-part for every customer we serve.

Our mission is to combine advanced metallurgy, modern CNC manufacturing, and rigorous quality control to create tools that outlast and outperform industry standards. We commit to responsive technical support, transparent pricing, and delivery timelines that keep our customers' production lines running.

Our Journey

Milestones That Shaped Us

From a 2-machine shop to a multi-crore export house in 15 years.

2008

Foundation

JK Overseas established in Moshi, Pune with 2 CNC grinding machines and a focus on single-point boring bars for the local automotive sector.

2011

Product Expansion

Launched carbide-tipped turning and boring tools. Facility expanded to 8,000 sq. ft. First export order fulfilled to a Bahrain distributor.

2014

ISO 9001 Certification

Achieved ISO 9001:2008 certification. Implemented CMM-based inspection for 100% dimensional verification of boring bars.

2017

Indexable Tooling Launch

Entered the indexable insert holder market. Partnered with carbide insert manufacturers for a complete CNC turning solution offering.

2019

New Manufacturing Wing

Expanded to 18,000 sq. ft. with PVD coating unit, heat treatment facility, and dedicated R&D lab. Team grew to 45+ personnel.

2021

20 Countries Milestone

Active export presence in 20 countries across Middle East, Africa, and Southeast Asia. Upgraded to ISO 9001:2015.

2023

Digital Transformation

Launched digital product catalog, online quotation system, and ERP-based order tracking for international clients.

2024

30+ Countries & Growing

Exceeded 30-country export milestone. 2,500+ product SKUs. Setting sights on European market entry and aerospace certification.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Principles that guide every decision — from tool design to client communication.

Precision First

Every micron matters. We hold ourselves to tighter tolerances than our customers require — because that's the only way to guarantee consistent performance in the field.

Uncompromised Quality

No shortcuts. No second-grade materials. ISO 9001-driven processes from raw material intake to final despatch inspection ensure every tool meets spec.

Customer Partnership

We don't sell tools — we solve machining problems. Our engineers stay engaged through application testing, failure analysis, and ongoing optimisation.

Continuous Innovation

We invest 8% of annual revenue back into R&D — new coating technologies, geometry developments, and digital tools that keep our clients ahead of competition.

Global Mindset

We think in international standards — DIN, ISO, ANSI. Our documentation, packaging, and support are designed for clients from Mumbai to Munich.

Integrity in Business

Transparent pricing. Honest lead times. If we can't do it, we say so. Our reputation is built on every quote we honour and every delivery we make on time.
