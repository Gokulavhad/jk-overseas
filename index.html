Who We Are

Engineering Precision, Delivering Trust

Founded in 2008, JK Overseas is a Pune-based manufacturer and global exporter of precision boring bars, carbide-tipped tools, and CNC machining solutions. We combine metallurgical expertise with modern manufacturing to deliver tools that perform in the most demanding industrial environments.

ISO 9001:2015 Certified

Every tool manufactured under strict quality management systems ensuring consistent dimensional accuracy and surface finish.

Global Export Network

Active export relationships across Europe, Middle East, Southeast Asia, and the Americas with seamless documentation support.

Custom Tool Engineering

Bespoke tool design and manufacturing for OEM clients with CAD-driven specifications and rapid prototyping capability.
