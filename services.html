What We Offer

Our Services

Four core service pillars that make JK Overseas more than a tooling supplier.

Technical Consultation

Our application engineers provide in-depth consultation on tool selection, cutting parameters, work-holding, and material-grade matching. Available via email, phone, and on-site visits for major accounts.

  • Work material and hardness assessment
  • Carbide grade and geometry recommendation
  • Cutting speed, feed, and depth optimisation
  • Bore tolerance and finish troubleshooting
  • Tool-life cost-per-part analysis
  • On-site machining trials for new applications

Custom Tool Design & Manufacture

When standard catalog tools won't reach your bore, match your fixture, or hit your tolerance — we design and manufacture bespoke tooling to your exact drawing or sample. Fast prototyping with full CMM inspection before despatch.

  • DWG / PDF drawing to finished tool in 10–15 days
  • Reverse engineering from physical samples
  • Special-profile boring bars and form tools
  • OEM private-label tooling
  • Prototype to production volume scaling
  • Full dimensional inspection report included

Installation & Setup Support

Getting a new boring bar or tooling system set up correctly on the machine is critical to achieving the rated performance. Our team provides setup guidelines, runout checks, and initial trial cutting support to ensure first-off success.

  • Machine interface and adaptor selection
  • Runout and concentricity check procedures
  • Initial trial cut parameter sheets
  • Video-call setup assistance included
  • On-site commissioning for large orders
  • Setup documentation in English and Hindi

After-Sales Support & Regrinding

Tool failure analysis, replacement recommendations, and carbide regrinding services extend the life of your tooling investment. We also offer scheduled supply contracts to eliminate emergency ordering and ensure uninterrupted production.

  • Boring bar regrinding and re-tipping service
  • Failure analysis reports with root cause
  • Replacement tool recommendation within 24h
  • Annual supply contracts with priority despatch
  • Consignment stock arrangements for high-volume users
  • Warranty replacement for manufacturing defects
